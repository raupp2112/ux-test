MATT'S COLLECTION

This collection is a technical study of transitional-era hardware. It focuses on the structural honesty, material tolerances, and mechanical intuition of devices from a time when tactile, physical components defined the user experience.

Navigation Protocol:
Select a specimen from the grid to enter its diagnostic view. Scroll to inspect internal layouts and orthogonal views. Hover over the canvas to use the magnifier tool for surface-level analysis, and access the data tables for precise material compositions and hardware schematics.
